Our Story

About Piece x Piece

Piece x Piece is a pop-up mosaic workshop designed for all skill levels. We offer an accessible, hands-on creative experience where everyone is welcome — no artistic background required.

Our workshops are thoughtfully designed to foster relaxation, creativity, and a sense of accomplishment, blending artistic expression with the calming benefits of art therapy.

Each session includes all the materials you need to create your own mosaic piece. Just bring yourself and enjoy the process of this ancient art form.

We look forward to creating with you.
